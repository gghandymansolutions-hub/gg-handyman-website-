Privacy Policy

Last revised: 2026

At GG Handyman Solutions, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy outlines the measures we take to safeguard your data when you visit our website, engage our services, or contact us in any way.

Information we collect

We may collect and store personal information that you provide us, such as your name, address, phone number, and email address. We use this information to provide you with our services, respond to your inquiries, and keep you informed of updates and promotions.

We also collect non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information, to improve our website's functionality and user experience. We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather statistical data on how our website is used.

How we use your information

We use your personal information only for the purposes stated in this policy or as required by law. We may use your information to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Process payments and invoices
  • Send you promotional materials and updates
  • Monitor and improve our website and services

We may share your personal information with our third-party service providers, such as payment processors and marketing partners, only as necessary to provide our services to you. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. We use secure servers, encryption, and other security protocols to safeguard your data. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Your rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information that we hold. You may also request that we restrict or object to the processing of your data, or withdraw your consent to receive marketing communications from us. To exercise your rights, please contact us at gghandymansolutions@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the revised policy on our website with the date of last revision. By continuing to use our website or services after the revisions are in effect, you agree to the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or data practices, please contact us at gghandymansolutions@gmail.com. We will do our best to address your inquiry as soon as possible.
