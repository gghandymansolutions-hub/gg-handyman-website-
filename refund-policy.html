Refund Policy

Last revised: 2026

At GG Handyman Solutions, we strive to provide high-quality home maintenance and repair services to our clients. If you are dissatisfied with our services, we offer a refund under certain circumstances, as outlined below.

Refund eligibility

Refunds are only available if you cancel your service appointment prior to the scheduled date and time. If we are unable to complete the work due to unforeseen circumstances, we will reschedule the appointment at no extra cost or issue a refund.

Once the work has been completed, refunds are not available. We stand behind the quality of our services and will work with you to resolve any issues that arise after the work is completed, but we cannot offer a refund for services that have already been rendered.

How to request a refund

To request a refund, please contact us at gghandymansolutions@gmail.com or call our office at (346) 504-9726. We will review your request and determine your eligibility for a refund.

Refund processing

If you are eligible for a refund, we will process your refund within 5–10 business days. We will refund the original payment method used for the transaction, unless otherwise agreed upon.

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please contact us at gghandymansolutions@gmail.com or call our office at (346) 504-9726. We will do our best to address your inquiry as soon as possible.
